My Biography

Hi, my name is Albert and i'm going to tell you all about myself. I was born on July 8th, 2003 and I like technology. I started to get into video games and tech around the age of 4, when I got a PS2, with several video games. Later on throughout the years I got more video games, and I started having responsibilities. Like taking care of my dog and my family, my parents went into a divorce for (censored) reasons in 2012, so I was forced into a situation of going back and forth, later on in 2013-14 I entered Middle School, and it wasn't easy to adapt to. Eventually I adapted, some of my friends from elementary were there and I managed to make new friends. My life was overbalanced with my parents divorce, it was until June 29 2016 that my world would fall to pieces, the day my dog died. That was the saddest day of my life, I had lost my lifetime best friend and brother a week before my birthday, now I wasn't there when he passed, I was at my fathers house. When I got the news I was so depressed that I stayed in my room for the rest of the day, holding his ashes. Now I am currently still left with a hole in my heart, but I try to distract my mind from it the best I can. I am a hard worker now, I want to do greater things for the world.
